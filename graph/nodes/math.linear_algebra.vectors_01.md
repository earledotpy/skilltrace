# Represent and combine vectors

## Learning target

Read a vector as an ordered list of numbers and as an arrow from the origin,
add two vectors component-by-component, and scale a vector by a constant.
Explain what addition and scaling do to the arrow.

## Study pointers

Start with 3Blue1Brown's *Essence of Linear Algebra*, chapter 1 ("Vectors,
what even are they?") for the geometric picture, then Khan Academy's
*Vectors* unit (Linear Algebra) for component arithmetic with worked answers.

## Notes

Foundational for every later linear-algebra node; the dot product, matrices,
and matrix products are all built on this representation.
