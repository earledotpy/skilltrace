# Work with percentages, ratios, and proportions

## Learning target

Convert fluently between fractions, decimals, and percentages, compute a percentage of a quantity and a percentage change, and solve a proportion by cross-multiplication.

## Study pointers

Khan Academy Arithmetic (or Pre-algebra), the "Ratios, rates, and percentages" unit, for worked problems with answers.

## Notes

Rates, proportions, and percentages are the everyday language of probabilities, growth, and normalized data.
