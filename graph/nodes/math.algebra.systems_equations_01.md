# Solve a system of two linear equations

## Learning target

Solve a system of two linear equations in two unknowns by substitution and by elimination, and interpret the solution as the point where the two lines meet (including no-solution and infinitely-many cases).

## Study pointers

Khan Academy Algebra, the "Systems of equations" unit, for worked substitution and elimination problems with answers.

## Notes

Systems are the algebraic root of the matrix equation Ax = b and of solving for several unknowns at once.
