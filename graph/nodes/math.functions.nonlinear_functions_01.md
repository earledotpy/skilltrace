# Recognize nonlinear function shapes

## Learning target

Recognize a quadratic (parabola) and an exponential curve from a graph or table, contrast their rate of change with a line's constant rate, and describe where each grows fastest.

## Study pointers

Khan Academy Algebra, "Quadratic functions" and "Exponential growth," and Precalculus for the shapes, with worked graph-reading problems.

## Notes

Nonlinearity is what activation functions and growth models add over linear ones; recognizing the shapes prepares that.
