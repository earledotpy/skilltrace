# Explain optimization by following the gradient

## Learning target

Explain how repeatedly stepping in the direction of the negative gradient descends toward a minimum, describe the role of step size, and name a failure case (local minimum, step too large).

## Study pointers

3Blue1Brown "Gradient descent, how neural networks learn" for the walking-downhill picture; Khan Academy, "Optimization" and "Maxima and minima," for the calculus grounding.

## Notes

Intuition only — this is the mental model of how every model is trained.
